Our Mission

At FC ALPHA, our mission is to discover, train, and elevate young football talent while instilling values of discipline, teamwork, and perseverance. We aim to create a legacy of excellence, one player at a time.

Our Story

The story of FC ALPHA began in 2012, when former professional footballer Ervin Hoxha founded the academy with a vision to nurture untapped talent. Starting with just 20 players on a modest field, we have grown into a leading institution with world-class facilities and a global reputation.

From regional championships to international collaborations, FC ALPHA has consistently proved that passion, dedication, and teamwork can achieve greatness.
